## Welcome to Our Research

This comprehensive analysis explores payment behavior and preferences among Gen Z and Alpha Gen Z consumers, revealing critical insights for merchants, payment providers, and financial institutions.

### 🎯 Quick Facts

- **Survey Respondents:** 2,800+ across 5 generational cohorts
- **Key Finding:** 65% of Gen Z prefer digital wallets for everyday purchases
- **BNPL Adoption:** 40% of Gen Z actively uses Buy Now Pay Later services
- **Top Priority:** Speed is #1 feature preference (beating security and privacy)
- **Mobile Dominance:** 78% prefer mobile-first checkout experiences

---

## 📊 Key Research Insights

### Payment Method Preferences

**Gen Z's Top 5 Payment Methods:**
1. 🔐 **Digital Wallets** (65% adoption)
   - Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay leading
   - Preferred for speed and security
   - Cross-device consistency matters

2. 💳 **Debit Cards** (45% regular use)
   - Control and immediate funds deduction
   - No debt accumulation concerns
   - Less popular among higher-income Gen Z

3. 💰 **Credit Cards** (35% active users)
   - Rewards and cashback attracting adoption
   - Lower adoption than older generations
   - Debt concerns limiting growth

4. 📱 **PayPal/Venmo** (42% active users)
   - Peer-to-peer payments dominant use case
   - Trust established over time
   - Broad merchant acceptance

5. ⏳ **Buy Now Pay Later** (40% active users)
   - Fastest growing payment method
   - Strong preference among price-conscious segments
   - Concerns about debt accumulation emerging

### BNPL Ecosystem Insights

**Buy Now Pay Later Awareness Funnel:**
- **Awareness:** 82% of Gen Z aware of BNPL
- **Active Users:** 40% actively using
- **Conversion Rate:** 49% of aware become active users
- **Satisfaction:** 4.1/5.0 average rating

**Top BNPL Providers (Gen Z):**
1. Klarna (32% of BNPL users)
2. Afterpay (28%)
3. Affirm (24%)
4. Other (16%)

**Key BNPL Concerns:**
- Risk of overspending (48%)
- Hidden fees (32%)
- Debt accumulation (61%)
- Lack of understanding (27%)
